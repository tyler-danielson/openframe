## Full Description (4000 chars max)

OpenFrame is a companion app for your self-hosted OpenFrame dashboard — a smart home display system that brings your calendar, tasks, photos, and smart home controls together on any screen.

**What you can do with OpenFrame:**

- View your daily agenda and upcoming events across Google and Microsoft calendars
- Create and manage events with natural language input
- Track tasks and to-do lists
- Browse and manage your photo albums
- Control and configure kiosk displays around your home
- Connect via QR code for instant, secure setup

**Key Features:**

Calendar & Events
• See today's schedule at a glance
• Browse events by week or month
• Create events quickly with natural language
• Sync with Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook

Task Management
• View and manage tasks across all your lists
• Mark tasks complete on the go

Photo Albums
• Browse your photo collections
• View album slideshows

Kiosk Management
• Monitor and control your OpenFrame displays
• Manage kiosk settings remotely

**Self-Hosted & Private**
OpenFrame connects to your own server — your data stays under your control. No cloud accounts required, no data collection, no ads.

**Getting Started**
1. Set up an OpenFrame server (see openframe.us/guide)
2. Open the app and enter your server URL
3. Log in with your credentials or scan a QR code
4. You're all set!
